TASK

Design a robot either wired or wireless within the specified dimensions that can operated manually and can travel through all turns of the track .The fastest and steady robot will be the winner.

BOT SPECIFICATIONS

  1. The maximum dimension of the robot can be 25 cm x 25cm x20 cm (l x b x h).
  2. Maximum weight must not exceed 5 kg.
  3. The maximum allowable operating voltage should not exceed 12 volts.
  4. The machine must not be made from Lego parts, or any ready-made kit, if we find such machine it will be disqualified.
  5. The participants will be provided with 220 Volts, 50Hz standard AC supply. Participants will have to arrange themselves arrange for power adaptor.
  6. Robot must not contain any readymade kits, pneumatic &hydraulic systems, IC engines.

TEAM RULES

  1. Every team must have a specific name and a representative name.
  2. Each team should have minimum of 3 members and maximum of 5 members for this event.
  3. All team members must carry a id proof showing their name and college name.

RULES AND REGULATION

  1. Team members will be allowed only two times to touch or reset their robots position during the run. However, this will lead to a time penalty and timer will not stop during this course of action.
  2. If the robot fall from the arena three times the team will be disqualified.
  3. Unethical behaviour could lead to disqualification.
  4. Event head and co-ordinators have all right to take final decision for any matter during the event.
  5. The robot will be judged on basis of (in priority):- a. Time to complete the track. b. Number of obstacle cleared.

COMPETITION ROUNDS

Round 1

  1. Every team can participate in this round. This round is an elimination round .Team will be ranked on basis of their individual time to cover the track. Top performers of this round will get into next round.
Round 2
  1. Co-ordinators will divide teams into groups. Each group contain two teams.
  2. This is a knockout round between the two teams in a same group, one who beats another team of the same group will go for the next round.
  3. The top team from round-2 makes it to the final round on basis of time and number of obstacle cleared.
