ROBO EMPLACING
INTRODUCTION

Design a robot either wired or wireless within the specified dimensions that can operated manually and can travel through all obstacles of the track .The smooth and steady Bot will be the winner.

BOT SPECIFICATIONS

  1. The maximum dimension of the Bot can be 25 cm x 25cm x 25 cm (l x b x h). (5% tolerance)
  2. Maximum weight must not exceed 5 kg.
  3. The maximum allowable operating voltage should not exceed 12 volts.
  4. The machine must not be made from Lego parts, or any ready-made kit, if we find such machine it will be disqualified.
  5. The participants will be provided with 220 Volts, 50Hz standard AC supply. Participants will have to arrange themselves arrange for power adaptor.
  6. Bot must not contain any readymade kits, pneumatic &hydraulic systems, IC engines.

TEAM RULES

  1. Every TEAM must have a specific name.
  2. Each team should have minimum of 3 members and maximum of 5 members for this event.
  3. All team members must carry a id proof showing their name and college name.

RULES AND REGULATION

  1. Team members will be allowed only two times to touch or reset their Bots position during the run. However, this will lead to a penalty.
  2. If the Bot fall from the arena two times the team will be disqualified.
  3. Unethical behaviour could lead to disqualification.
  4. Judges and co-ordinators have all right to take final decision for any matter during the event.
  5. Participants will be judged on basis of (in priority):- a. Points for parking. b. Number of obstacle cleared. c. Smooth driving.
  6. No restrictions on wheel sizes, types, or numbers.
  7. Purposely damaging track or college property will lead to disqualification and Authorities will take proper action.

COMPETITION ROUNDS

Round 1

  1. Every team can participate in this round. This round is an elimination round .Team will be ranked on basis of their individual score of their performance. Top performers of this round will get into next round.
Round 2
  1. Co-ordinators will divide teams into groups. Each group contain two teams.
  2. This is a knockout round between the two teams in a same group, one who beats another team of the same group will go for the next round.
  3. The top team from round-2 makes it to the final round on basis of total points.

the evaders
INTRODUCTION

Design a automatic ROBOT that has to avoid the obstacles present in the track. One team will compete at a time.

BOT SPECIFICATIONS

  1. The robot must fit within a 20cm x 20cm x 20cm.
  2. Bots must be strictly autonomous.
  3. Power supply must be strictly on the board and should not exceed 12V DC.
  4. Maximum number of sensors allowed is 1.

RULES AND REGULATION

  4. All motors must be 60 RPM
  5. A team of maximum 2 players are allowed.
  6. For every obstacle the bot touches , penalty of 5secs will be added to the actual time taken to complete the task.

Clash of bots
INTRODUCTION

  1. Build a manual controlled robot designed to take on other robots.
  2. Be prepare to face-off or kick down or smash other bot out of the ring or can lift bot upside down (flip).
  3. The bot should be capable enough to survive the obstacle given on the arena.
  4. Every time the best Survivor team will be awarded with points.
  5. The bot should have proper ground clearance with the arena.
  6. Teams that are not ready when called for battle will be considered to be declared as walkover & will receive no points.
  7. In all cases ,the co-ordinator team decision will be final decision.
  8. For the wire bots care should be taken so that the wires do not pull / entangled.

BOT SPECIFICATIONS

  1. The Riot bot should be within the weight limit of 5kg, with 1-2% tolerance. Size-30 cm*30 cm*30 cm.
  2. The bot should be designed as per simple wedge-lifter Mechanism.
  3. No spinner/ cutter/ hammer / drum should be used.
  4. Robots are not allowed which are constructed using lego kits or any other ready made mechanism.
  5. Power connection should be wired or wireless.
  6. Any kind of flame based weapon or ignitable solid or liquid projectiles weapons are not allowed.
  7. Using any kind of magnet in the bot is strictly prohibited.
  8. Make sure that your bot must not harm the arena.

TEAM SPECEFICATIONS

  1. A team may consist minimum of three and maximum five to six members.
  2. There should be entry fee if the team contains participant from different institute.
  3. The team must have some unique name for recognition.
  4. No bots can be shared between two teams.
  5. The participants will be provided standard 220V-60HZ AC supply. Thereafter they can use standard Battery or SMPS for DC supply.

EVENT SPECIFICATIONS

  1. There will be two rounds for two robots to participate at a time.
  2. 1 round of 3min will be played.
  3. Each robot will be allotted a particular spot.
  4. Groups competing have to strictly follow the rules, else will be disqualified.
  5. The decision of the Event head will be final.
  6. Penalty for Arena destruction.

RULES AND REGULATION

  1. All teams should have their bots inspected by the coordinators both before and after each stage.
  2. All members should produce their valid College id card.
  3. Teams are permitted to make minor changes to the body of their robot after every stage if they wish; but modifying the entire body of the robot will not be permitted.
  4. Arena will be disclosed only at the time of the event.
  5. Coordinators can make changes to the arena at any point of time.
  6. Any team/team member found disrespecting the rules/other competitors, will be disqualified.
  7. Bonus points will be announced one hour prior to the event.
  8. Coordinators can change the event format at any time during the event.
  9. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
  10. Malpractices will lead to immediate disqualification.
  11. Organizers reserve the right to make decisions in case of conflicts and other issues.
  12. The program committee reserves the right to deduct from the prize money, if expected number of teams have not participated (min 8 teams)
  13. Judge's decision will be final.
  14. A certificate of participation will be awarded for all the participants.

wall-e
INTRODUCTION

To build an automated robot which follows a line on its given path.

BOT SPECIFICATIONS

  1. .The robot must fit within a 20cm*20cm*20cm
  2. Bots must be strictly autonomous.
  3. Power supply must be strictly below 12v dc.
  4. Maximum numbers of sensors allowed is 6.
  5. Readymade bots are not allowed.

TRACK SPECIFICATIONS

  1. A pvc flex sheet of white color with a designed black path is used for the arena. Bots have to follow the black path only.
  2. The track will have right and acute angles, junctions, curves and circles.
  3. Width of the black line is 3-5cm.
  4. The track will be placed appropriately with ambient lighting.

MARKING SCHEME

  1. TIME: Marks will be awarded from 100 to 0 serially in an descending order of the rank obtained with a difference of 100 divided by numbers of teams.
  2. OBJECTIVE: Marks will be deduced on the number of times the bot goes of track(2 seconds for each instant) and number of human touches required(5 seconds for each instant)after the timer has been started with a limitation of 5 touches.
  3. INNOVATION AND DESIGN: Maximum of 5 marks for each innovation and designs will be awarded by the jury. The decision of the jury will be irrefutable.
  4. POWER CONSUMPION: Maximum of 2 marks for power consumption will be awarded by the jury. The decision of the jury will be irrefutable.

TEAM RULES

  1. Any team can participate in the line follower event. each team will consist of 1-2members.

RULES AND REGULATION

  1. Minimum participants are 1 and maximum are 2
  2. The robot which will complete the track within least time will be declared as winner.
  3. A bot causing any types of damage to the arena will be disqualified.
  4. The same bot cannot be used by more than one teams and the same team cannot play again with any other name and bot.
  5. Unethical behaviour could lead to disqualification.
  6. In case of a tie the decision of the event heads will be finalized.
  7. Event head and co-ordinators have all right to take final decision for any matter during the event

COMPETITION ROUNDS

Round 1

  1. This is an elimination round and the teams will run their bots on the given track respective to their alloted sequence.
  2. The teams will be ranked in accordance to the score they are awarded by the jury.
  3. Top 4 teams will proceed to the next round.
Round 2
  1. This is a knock out round and the teams will be grouped with 2 teams in each group.
  2. The best of each group will compete between each other.
  3. And the winner will be declared as the winner of the competition.
