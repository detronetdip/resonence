FILMWALA
RULES AND REGULATION

  1. The content shouldn't contain any nudity or controversy.
  2. DURATION: The films shall not be more than 20 minutes , including the intro and end credits. Films exceeding this time limit are liable to be rejected. The minimum length should be 6 minutes including credits.
  3. You have to submit the story and final clip within due time.
  4. FORMAT: The films must be in MP4 format. The recommended screen ratio is 16:9 .
  5. COPYRIGHT: The Film must not contain any copyrighted work.
  6. The Short Film must be based on any subject or concept. No Documentary film content will be accepted.
  7. Maximum cast and crew of the short film must be college students of facilities.
  8. Silent, talkies and animated films all are allowed.

MEME O PEDIA
INTRODUCTION

Do you have what it takes to bring meme creation to the next level? Cos “It's The Meme Fizz That Does The Bizz” right so .... it's time to make it happen. RULES AND REGULATION

  1. This is an individual competition for each participant they have to create their own meme post or video and should post it on their own social media platform like Facebook or Instagram.
  2. The material of meme post should not be any vulnerable content or nudity , political jokes or offensive meme.If such founds participants will be disqualified immediately.
  3. Participants required to post their meme on their social media platform and they should use #memeopedia tag for caption.
  4. Each participant can Post only 4 posts in .jpg and .jpeg format and including videos which should not extend 5mb.
  5. Creativity of the Meme will be 50% Quality of the Meme's Message will be 45% Amount of reactions of the meme received - 5%
  6. The memes which have the highest reaction or which will be the most funniest one surveyed by the coordinators will be considered as winner.
  7. Notes:-
    • Every participants meme should be unique not copied from any idea or from the orginals.
    • Top memes will be shared from the resonancexi official site.
