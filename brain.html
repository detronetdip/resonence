weighbridge
INTRODUCTION

Students will design and construct a model bridge that will carry a maximum load by using maximum 200 Popsicle sticks (ice-cream sticks).

RULES AND REGULATION

  1. COMPOSITION OF TEAMS: (Minimum-3, Maximum-5) students per team.
  2. Building Materials :
    • Popsicle sticks (maximum 200 Popsicle sticks) and White Glue will be provided by us.
    • pinning, clamping or non-white glue are not allowed to be used for connections.Failure to abide by this rule will result in disqualification.
    • The bridge must have clearance to insert the loading bar.
  3. Dimensions :
    • Maximum horizontal length= 65cm ,minimum= 55cm.
    • Maximum width= 12cm, minimum= 10cm.
    • Maximum height above top of the roadway= 12cm, minimum= 10cm.
  4. Time: 5 hours.
